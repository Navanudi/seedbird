<h2 dir="ltr">WHAT IS A PROJECT PARTNER?</h2>
<p dir="ltr">Project Partners fund entire projects ranging from educational to agriculture initiatives, changing the lives of thousands of people.</p>
<p dir="ltr"></p>
<h3 dir="ltr">WHY SPONSOR A PROJECT?</h3>
<p dir="ltr">Our community of project partners support and drives the humanitarian work we do. These inspiring individuals partner with Seed Bird Association to fund entire projects because they want to know that their generosity is being used strategically, in a scalable and sustainable way. We spend every rupee of their donations to empower teachers to reorganise school as a place to experience the real world for children, and farmers to go back to natural farming to save seed and soil to flourish, ensuring that their generosity is most efficiently used as a catalyst for change.</p>
<p dir="ltr">Leave your mark on the world in a positive way by spreading wealth to those in need. Their lives will be changed for the good, as will yours.</p>
<p dir="ltr">PROCESS</p>
<p dir="ltr">Once their donation has been put to good use, we provide our program partners with regular and frequent updates about the children, teachers and farmers they support. We connect them to the community they’ve helped through tangible, quantifiable and real examples of positive growth. Through these updates, they get to know the people they’ve helped and the steady growth they’re making</p>
<p dir="ltr">PARTICIPATION</p>
<p dir="ltr"></p>
<p dir="ltr">Finally, we also foster a community between our donors, bringing these special, like-minded individuals together through their common benevolence and generosity. We recognize these benefactors at an elevated level, communicating the enormous impact they have made on our supporters.Giving it your all.We have no middleman, we hire and train local staff to implement our projects. The Seed Bird Association team is on the ground, ensuring that your donation rupees go where they’re needed most. What’s more, giving back forever connects you to a community. We’re committed to complete honesty and transparency with our donor partners.</p>
