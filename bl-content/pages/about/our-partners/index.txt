<p dir="ltr"><strong>LPCT GUJARATI VIDYALAYAM </strong></p>
<p dir="ltr">LPCT Gujarati Vidyalayam is a government-aided school which was established in the 1960s in 1 town, Vijayawada by Sree Vijayawada  Gujarati Samaj.  Children from LKG to 10th class with poor economic backgrounds study here and the school is catering for the educational needs of 550 children. </p>
<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;"><strong>VANAJA CHANDRA PUBLIC SCHOOL, GADAVALLI</strong><span id="docs-internal-guid-233e9e97-7fff-c892-ab84-c9e553372e0b"></span></p>
<p dir="ltr">This school is established by NAUDAMMA FOUNDATION in 1992. In Gudavalli ,Cherukupalli Mandal, Guntur dt.  Children from LKG to 10th class with different economic and rural backgrounds study here and the school's total strength is around 1300.</p>
<p dir="ltr">Out of these 1300 children nearly 600 children are studying in nursery, LKG, UKG and 1st class under a program named ANKURAM which is designed and developed by SEED BIRD.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong>HEAL PARADISE, THOTAPALLY</strong></p>
<p dir="ltr">It is a school for orphan and destitute children which is located in Thotapalli, Agiripalli Mandal, Krishna Dt. Along with education HEAL also provides food and boarding facilities. Children from 1st class to 12th class study here and the school's total strength is around 600. </p>
<p dir="ltr">Nearly 300 children are studying in primary school. We have been training teachers and working with children of primary for the past 3years.  Academic concepts are integrated with different activities like games, arts, theatre, storytelling, farming, cooking etc and we have seen significant changes in the children.</p>
